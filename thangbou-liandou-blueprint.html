The Origin

A folktale of the Paite Tribe
— reborn as a venture

In the misty hills of Churachandpur, Manipur, among the Paite people of Northeast India, there is a story passed from generation to generation — the tale of two brothers, Thanghou and Liandou.

These brothers did not simply share bread. They shared even what was smallest — the very grain of a mustard seed split in two. Their love was not a grand gesture; it was a thousand quiet ones. It was the belief that no portion of goodness is too small to give, and no bond too tender to protect.

Today, that spirit is the foundation of this venture. Project Thanghou-Liandou is named after them — a joint mission between Educafe and Flowpandas — where every profit, every equity stake, every student reached is a mustard seed shared between two hands that refuse to let go.
